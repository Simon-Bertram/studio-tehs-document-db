# Record a business

## Goal

Capture a historical business with owners and places of business.

## Steps

1. Open **Taxonomies & Entities → Businesses** and create a new document.
2. Set **Business Name** (required).
3. Link **Owners / Operators** to Historical Person documents.
4. Under **Place of Business**, reference one or more **Locations** and/or **Properties**.
5. Publish when ready.

## Field notes

- Create person and place entities first if they do not already exist.
- Places can mix villages (locations) and specific buildings (properties).
