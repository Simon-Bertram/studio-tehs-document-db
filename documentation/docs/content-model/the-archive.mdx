# The Archive

The Archive holds primary historical media: transcribed clippings and letters, and cataloged photographs. Prefer creating reusable people, places, and categories under Taxonomies & Entities, then link them here.

## Primary Sources / Transcriptions

Use a **Primary Source** for a single historical piece of media (newspaper ad, letter, notice)—not for modern essays.

Key field groups:

- **Identity** — `docId` (legacy, unique), title, textual and/or exact dates, publication name
- **Content** — scan image, full transcription, sheriff’s sale flag and legal writ
- **Place** — associated property (preferred) or standalone township; people mentioned
- **Research** — subject categories and Quarterly citations

Related: [Add a primary source](../common-tasks/add-primary-source.mdx), [Enter a sheriff’s sale](../common-tasks/enter-sheriff-sale.mdx)

## Historical Images

Use a **Historical Image** for photographs with provenance and rights metadata.

Key field groups:

- **Identity** — `identifier` (legacy, unique), serial number, caption, date taken
- **Content** — required photograph file, full description
- **Place** — specific location (preferred) or township
- **Provenance / Rights** — source, contributor, donation, photographer, rights, archivist notes
- **Research** — subjects and Quarterly citations

Related: [Catalog a historical image](../common-tasks/catalog-historical-image.mdx)
